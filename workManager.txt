Work Manger is Guaranteed execution thats defferable.

.Guaranteed constraint aware execution
.Respectful of system background restrictions
.Backwards compatible with or without google play services
.Queryable
.Chainable
.Opportunistic

**Core classes**

.Worker (this is the class that does the work)
.WorkRequest
   .OneTimeWorkRequest (This is for things to be done once)
   .PeriodicWorkRequest (This is for recurrig work)

Example of PeriodicWorkRequests:
  
Periodically backup your data, download fresh content in your app,
or upload logs to a server.
